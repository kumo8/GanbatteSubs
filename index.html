About

Ganbatte Subs uses English English and does not include Japanese words in the script such as honorifics or other terms of address. I kindly ask anyone who has an objection to the lack of Japanese in English to please learn Japanese as it is the clear remedy to your problem.

Ganbatte Subs will return for a season 2 of Aguu should it ever get made.

Aguu -Tensai Ningyou-

Show Info

Synopsis:

Ai and her best friend Machi are both aspiring professional ballet dancers. Machi possesses exceptional skill at ballet and Ai looks up to her as a role model. Ai is always struggling to improve and sometimes even feels a bit of jealously towards her. One day, Ai learns about the mysterious entities called "Aguu" that grant genius-level talent to those that possess them, the tailors who create such entities, and the saviours who seek to stop the tailors. What do the Aguu mean for Ai and Machi, and how will they fare as they are thrust into the midst of the struggle between tailors and saviours?
