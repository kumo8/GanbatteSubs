About

Ganbatte Subs uses English English and does not include Japanese words in the script such as honorifics or other terms of address. I kindly ask anyone who has an objection to the lack of Japanese in English to please learn Japanese as it is the clear remedy to your problem.

Ganbatte Subs was founded as a one-person project but picked up some kind help along the way. It was the first fansub project I had ever worked on. It taught me a lot about how to translate, how to time, how to encode, and how to fit everything together in a coherent and pleasing way.

Ganbatte Subs will return for a season 2 of Aguu should it ever get made.

Ganbatte Subs might return if another unlicensed show catches my attention and I find enough motivation to do it. Another possibility is that I may simply join a more established group with better infrastructure.

Thank you for watching.

Show Info

Synopsis:

In the story, Ai is an ordinary girl, and a dancer. Her friend Machi is also a dancer, but Machi is a genius. Watching Machi's talent often makes Ai feel down. But one day, Ai finds out Machi's secret: the existence of the Aguu, palm-size entities who grant genius talent upon those who hold them. In order to save Machi, who has become a "Seamstress" who makes Aguu, Ai becomes a "Savior." The Saviors are a group of people who have fought the Seamstresses for generations.
